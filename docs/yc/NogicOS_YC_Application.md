## 一、YC 叙事方案

### 1.1 One-liner 候选

| 版本 | 内容 | 角度 |
|------|------|------|
| **A** | The first AI that works across your browser, files, and desktop as one. | 上下文 |
| **B** | AI that remembers how you work—and gets faster every time. | 体验 |
| **C** | The AI work partner that learns from everyone. | 网络效应 |

### 1.2 推荐组合

**Headline:**

> **"NogicOS: The AI that works where you work"**
> 
> 在你工作的地方工作的 AI

**Subline:**

> Browser. Files. Desktop. One AI, complete context. Gets faster every time.

### 1.3 核心叙事（1 分钟 Pitch）

1. **问题：** 知识工作者的工作分散在浏览器、本地文件、各种应用里。现有 AI 只能看到一个窗口。

2. **洞察：** 这不是 AI 能力的问题，是上下文获取的问题。Cursor 解决了程序员的这个问题。但知识工作者呢？

3. **解决方案：** NogicOS 是第一个能同时看到浏览器、文件和桌面的 AI。它不只是聊天，它能直接在你的工作环境里行动。

4. **护城河：** 而且它会学习。第一次做一个任务 30 秒，第二次 1 秒。用的人越多，所有人越快。

5. **愿景：** 我们要让每一个知识工作者都有一个 AI 伙伴，它理解你的工作，记住你的习惯，帮你越做越快。

### 1.4 叙事层次

```
Level 1 - 功能层：
  "AI that can control browser, files, and desktop"
  
Level 2 - 价值层：
  "AI that understands your complete work context"
  
Level 3 - 体验层：
  "AI that remembers how you work"
  
Level 4 - 愿景层：
  "The more people use it, the faster for everyone"
```

## 五、YC 申请表填写参考

### 5.1 公司描述（Describe what your company does）

> NogicOS is the first AI that works across your browser, files, and desktop as one unified workspace. While ChatGPT only sees what you paste, and Cursor only sees your code, NogicOS sees your complete work environment—and takes action directly in it.

### 5.2 问题（What problem are you solving?）

> Knowledge workers spend 80% of their time switching between browser, local files, and desktop apps. Every time they ask AI for help, they waste 5 minutes explaining context. Cursor solved this for developers by understanding their entire codebase. We're solving it for everyone else.

### 5.3 解决方案（How do you solve it?）

> NogicOS integrates browser control (Playwright), local file access, and desktop understanding into a single AI agent. It can navigate websites, read/write files, and understand your screen—completing end-to-end tasks that span multiple platforms.

### 5.4 差异化（What makes you different?）

> 1. **Unified Context**: First to connect browser + files + desktop in one AI
> 2. **Gets Faster**: Smart Router learns from your usage—30s first time, 1s next time
> 3. **Network Effect**: Knowledge Store shares learnings across users—more users = faster for everyone
> 4. **Local-First**: Your data stays on your machine
